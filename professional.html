Professional

The Professional range makes your choice easy, you know you are getting the best products at the best prices.

Comprehensive / Durable / Specialist

The Professional range is our most extensive product offering aimed at applications that need to balance appearance, function and value for money. Our extensive grab rail range is tried and tested and delivers excellent quality and durability. It also contains our most comprehensive range of hinged support rails, shower seats and accessories, and even specialist products such as our Anti Ligature range.

If you cannot find exactly what you need in this range, our sales team would be happy to discuss your specific requirements.

1 YEAR STANDARD WARRANTY

Bathex offer a minimum 1 Year Warranty on all products.

3 YEAR STAINLESS WARRANTY

Bathex offer an enhanced 3 Year Warranty on all Stainless Steel products.

Concealed Fix

Most of the designs incorporate concealed fixings for enhanced visual styling. Available in a choice of polyester coated colours and both mirror and sateen polished finishes.

Extended Range

We offer a comprehensive range of hinged support rails and shower seats. We also have a collection of complementary accessories including towel rails, toilet roll holders, shower and other bathroom fixings.

Specialist Solutions

We offer a selection of standard components for installers to create matching solutions for difficult scenarios and situations. Also available is a range of Anti Ligature products designed to be used in areas with vulnerable people.
