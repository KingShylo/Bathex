Supporting major supply contracts

Many of our customers are supporting major contracts for large UK based organisations. These contracts can be challenging, your customers want quality, value for money and often something that’s just a little different. Contract tenders always seem to be on tight deadlines, the specifications arrive late and you are always under pressure to get the tender returned.

There are four main ways that Bathex can support you in this situation:

Meeting the Specification

You can be sure that all our products are manufactured to the highest quality and meet all the relevant standards. Products are manufactured here in the UK and you are always welcome to visit and take a look at our facilities.



That ‘Special’ Component

So you’ve looked for what you need and it is not in the catalogue.

Pick up the phone and our Sales Team will be happy to help, indeed we may already have a solution for you. If you do need something special or new we can design and sample products for you. On average, we complete special designs and get quotes to you in around a week.

If you need samples of special products we can normally get these to you in a couple of weeks.



Manufacture to Demand

If you are successful and win the supply contract we can continue to make a difference. We manufacture your contracts on demand, this means you don’t have to hold stock and we can take the guess work out of forecasting customer needs. We know that this will shorten supply times and ensure the highest levels of on-time in-full supply.



UK Supply

Stop having to worry about weather, customs, international holidays, currency, shipping costs or air freight. We are based in Birmingham and deliver 99.5% of shipments in the week that they are ordered.
