Distinction

Exclusive designs, superior materials and beautiful finishes in both contemporary and traditional styling.

Exclusive / Designer / Modern

The Distinction range consists of two main product families, our contemporary Yardley design with a modern angular appearance and the more traditional Knowle design with its classic curves. Both families benefit from the same graceful, low-profile fixturing.

Whatever your style preference our primary products are complemented by a selection of accessories that will grace any installation. For high frequency use in public environments consider the robust 35mm products, whereas for domestic applications with less intensive use take a look at the sleeker 25mm versions. All of these premium products are backed by our 10 year warranty and are available in a range of beautiful finishes and a wide range of colour options.

10 YEAR DISTINCTION WARRANTY

Bathex offer an extended 10 Year Warranty for all the Distinction range products.

Yardley

A contemporary design with a modern angular appearance. Part of the Yardley range are our new Arch and Curve rails that both benefit from the use natural curves to create a perfect ergonomic fit.

Available as a robust 35mm or a sleeker 25mm for more individual applications and domestic use.

Knowle

A modern twist on a classic design combined with low profile mounting plates and projection for when space is at a minimum.

Available as a robust 35mm or a sleeker 25mm for more individual applications and domestic use.

Accessories

A selection of products that can be used in combination with both our Yardley and Knowle designs to provide a matching solution for any high quality installations.
